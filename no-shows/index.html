Frequently Asked Questions

What if customers find the reminders annoying?

They don't. Customers appreciate the reminders because they help them avoid missing appointments. You can customize frequency and tone for different customer types.

How does the auto-fill feature work?

You maintain a standby list of customers waiting for openings. When someone cancels, system texts the standby list with job details. First confirmed response gets the slot.

Can customers reschedule on their own?

Yes. They can reply "Reschedule" and get a link to available time slots. They pick a new time, system updates your calendar, everyone's happy.

What calendars does this work with?

Google Calendar, Outlook, Apple Calendar, and most popular scheduling tools. Two-way sync keeps everything updated automatically.

How do I build a standby list?

System identifies customers with flexible schedules and automatically adds them to standby lists based on service type and location. You can also add customers manually.
