Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to all apps published by Jumpgate Industries LLC under the "SnapStack" brand, including but not limited to BottleSnap, BladeSnap, and BrickSnap (collectively, the "Apps").

1. Information We Collect

Photos you scan: When you use the camera or photo library to identify an item, the image is sent to our servers for AI analysis. We process the image in real-time and do not permanently store it on our servers. Images are deleted after processing is complete.

Identification results: We may store your scan results (item name, value, category) to provide your collection/history features. This data is associated with an anonymous device identifier, not your personal identity.

Subscription data: Subscription purchases are processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store) and RevenueCat. We receive confirmation of your subscription status but do not have access to your payment details.

Analytics: We collect anonymous usage data (screens viewed, features used, crash reports) to improve the app experience. This data cannot identify you personally.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide AI-powered item identification
  • To maintain your scan history and collection
  • To manage your subscription status
  • To improve our identification accuracy and app experience

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

  • OpenAI: Images are sent to OpenAI's API for identification processing, subject to OpenAI's Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat: Anonymous subscription management
  • Analytics providers: Anonymous usage statistics

4. Data Retention

Photos are processed in real-time and not permanently stored. Scan history is retained until you delete it or uninstall the app. Subscription records are maintained as required for billing purposes.

5. Your Rights

You can delete your scan history within the app at any time. To request deletion of all data associated with your device, contact us at privacy@jumpgateindustries.com.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Apps are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. BottleSnap is rated 17+ due to alcohol-related content.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes through the app or by updating the "Last updated" date.
